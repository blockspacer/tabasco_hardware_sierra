digitStringToBCDString

Convert a digit string to BCD string (CRSM format) As per 3GPP TS11.11, a phone number in a digit string is converted in BCD.

Digit string(0-9) to BCD:: two digits are packets in a single byte, digit 1 is stored in 4 least significant bits of byte 1 and digit 2 is stored in 4 most significant bits of byte 1, similarly digit 3 in 4 LSbs of byte 2 and digit 4 in 4 MSbs of byte 2. Unknown digit is shown as 1111 binary
